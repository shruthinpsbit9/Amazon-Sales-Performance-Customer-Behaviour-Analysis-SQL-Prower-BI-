## Overview
Developed a 3-tab Power BI report to analyze Amazon sales data and identify key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and operational inefficiencies across categories and products.

## Key Analysis
- Designed a 3-layer dashboard (Business Overview, Product Category Analysis, Individual Product Analysis)  
- Performed Year-over-Year (YoY) growth analysis using DAX  
- Analyzed customer demand, delivery efficiency, and pricing vs revenue relationships  
- Evaluated customer dissatisfaction drivers and product-level performance

## Insights
- Health & Beauty and Fashion contribute over 55% of total demand  
- Electronics shows highest growth (~47%) but lower revenue efficiency  
- Customer dissatisfaction is driven by product quality issues (~5.8K–6.1K cases)  
- Delivery performance is consistent (~9.5 days) across categories  
- Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of high-performing products
